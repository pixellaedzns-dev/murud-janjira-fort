💡 Good to Know

Travel Notes

Budget Style

Calculated for a low-cost solo / backpacker trip using sleeper class and MSRTC ordinary buses.

Save More

Travel in Sleeper class, use MSRTC ordinary buses, eat at local dhaba / mess, and carry your own water bottle.

Accommodation

Cheapest dorm option included above. Book ahead on weekends and around the October–March season.

Best For

A 3-day, 2-night solo trip to one of India's strongest sea forts. Travel low, experience more.

🎒 Before You Go

Low Budget Travel Tips

🎫 Book train tickets early for Sleeper class.
🍱 Carry some home food and snacks.
🚌 Use public transport — MSRTC buses.
💬 Bargain while taking an auto or boat.
💵 Keep cash on hand — ATMs are limited in Murud.
⏰ Start early to save time and money.
📜

Historical Fact — Janjira Fort is one of the strongest sea forts in India, never captured by any enemy till date.
