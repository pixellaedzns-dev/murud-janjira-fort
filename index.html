Travel Plan — 3 Days / 2 Nights

Day 1Departure
  • Start from Kerala (night)
  • Board train to Mumbai / Panvel
  • Overnight journey
Day 2Arrival to Murud
  • Reach Mumbai / Panvel (morning)
  • Take MSRTC bus to Murud via Mangaon
  • Reach Murud (afternoon)
  • Visit Murud Beach & local area
  • Stay in a budget room or dormitory
Day 3The fort & return
  • Visit Janjira Fort by boat (morning)
  • Explore Murud market
  • Return to Panvel by bus
  • Board train to Kerala (night)
Day 4Early morning
  • Reach Kerala

Travel Tips — Low Budget

  • → Book train tickets early for Sleeper class.
  • → Carry some home food and snacks.
  • → Use public transport — MSRTC buses.
  • → Bargain while taking an auto or boat.
  • → Keep cash on hand — ATMs are limited in Murud.
  • → Start early to save time and money.
